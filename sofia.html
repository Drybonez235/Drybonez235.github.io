Sofia

Sofia is the capital of Bulgaria. I was able to explore this city and see many famous sites! Sofia is one of the most popular cities to visit when in Bulgaria simply because of the long history that the city holds. In Europe, most cities were established hundreds of years ago and have slowly evolved with new technological inovations. An example of this is city streets. Most streets are narrow and ideal for walking or horse and carriges, but not well suited for modern cars. Parking is hard to find!

Church of St Nicolus

This is a very famous Eastern Orthodox church in the Capitol of Bulgaria. It was built around 1200 A.D and is one of the oldest buildings in Bulgaria.

Monument to the Tsar Liberator

This is a monumnet dedicated to Alaxander II who liberated bulgaria from Ottoman rule in the Turkish war of 1877.
