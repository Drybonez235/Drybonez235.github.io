My Trip To Bulgaria

In the summer of 2017 I, along with a group of other college students went to Bulgaria. We went to help a local church with their summer youth program. The program ran for a week focused on choir and vocal performance. In all, 18 children particpated in the program.

While in Bulgaria, we stayed in Varna but we were able to visit Sophia, Nessbar, and Tsareverts Fortress.

This website will take you on a tour a few of these places and show you a few of the sites I got to experience!
